Burger Recipe

In this recipe i will give you all the information needed to make your own delicious burger.

Recipe

  • Hamburger patty
  • Bun
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Your favorite brand of burger sauce
  • Slice of cheddar

How to prepare

  1. put the meat in the pan with butter or oil for 5 minutes.
  2. slice the tomato and lettuce
  3. Add everything together and enjoy your burger
